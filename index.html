Le Nemours

Our Story

Le Nemours captures the essence with the spectacular view of Eiffel Tower and the restaurant has built its reputation over the past 20 years. Consistently delivering excellent food and faultless service, it’s no wonder. The menu showcases the finest local produce,which is a Le Nemours speciality.

“Food is one part of the experience. And it has to be somewhere between 50 to 60 percent of the dining experience. But the rest counts as well: The mood, the atmosphere, the music, the feeling, the design, the harmony between what you have on the plate and what surrounds the plate.” – Alain Ducasse, Chef.
